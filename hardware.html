Instructions

Step 1: Gather all the PVC peices

Gather all the PVC peices together. Set aside the 2 threaded plugs and two split joint nuts, which will be used later.

Step 2: Cut your PVC pipe to size

You will need the following sizes (need to verify all sizes)
  • 1x 10'
  • 1x 7'
  • 2x 6"
  • 2x 4"

    • Step 3: Cement the PVC together

    Following the instructions on the container, cement each peice of CPC together per the below diagram. Make sure that the upper and lower pvc tees and arms are fully aligned.

    Step 4: Drill out the threaded plug

    Using the drill and 1/2" spade bit, drill holes through the bottom of each plug.

    Step 5: Prepare the toslink cable

    For each of the two toslink cables:
  • Cut off one of the two connectors
  • Strip ~2" of the outer sheild off the toslink cable using the wire strippers
  • Clip the end off the cut side of the cable using the fiber optic cutters. Visually inspect the cable to ensure a clean cut
  • Thread the toslink cable through the threaded cap until the toslink connector is seated in the cap as shown

    • Step 6: Thread the toslink in the light collection unit
